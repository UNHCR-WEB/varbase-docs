## Create a Blog Page:

Follow these steps to create a new **Blog** __ page in your website:

1. Select **Add Content** from the **Manag**_e_ administrative menu**.**
2. Select __ **Blog page**_._
3. Fill in with all required data that is needed.
4. Click **Save**.&#x20;
